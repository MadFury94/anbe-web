About ANBE

An indigenous engineering house built for the Niger Delta's toughest work.

Incorporated in 1990, ANBE Nigeria Limited provides pipeline construction, fabrication engineering, and flare system design to operators across Nigeria's oil & gas sector. Our fabrication and maintenance facility, combined with a technical team drawn from combustion engineering and equipment maintenance disciplines, lets us take a project from design through procurement to commissioning without handing it off.

  • MissionDeliver engineering solutions of international standard, executed by Nigerian talent.
  • VisionBecome a leading Pipeline, Engineering & Construction company across West Africa.
  • SafetyEvery scope is planned, permitted, and closed out against a documented HSE standard.
  • ReliabilityBudgets and schedules are backed by real project data, not estimates alone.
